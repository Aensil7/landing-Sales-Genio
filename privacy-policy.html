What is Sales Genio?

Sales Genio is a customer relationship management (CRM) tool for WhatsApp Business powered by artificial intelligence. The product offers a “Conversation Cloud Platform” with WhatsApp Business that allows our customers to store, manipulate, analyze, and transfer messages between their business systems and their customers across various messaging channels provided by Sales Genio and third parties (the “Service”).Sales Genio is committed to the protection of personal data, including the data we use for our own purposes and that we maintain on behalf of our customers.

Cloud API

‍
Sales Genio sends messages to the cloud API. The cloud API service temporarily stores the messages. It then handles sending the message to the WhatsApp platform. Initially, the messages are stored for any necessary transmission. WhatsApp messages are then automatically deleted after 30 days.

Cookie Policy

‍
Sales Genio uses cookies and similar technologies to record log data. We use both session-based and persistent cookies.

Cookies are small text files that we send to your computer and from your computer or mobile device to us each time you visit our website or use our desktop application. They are unique to your account or your browser. Session-based cookies last only while your browser is open and are automatically deleted when you close your browser. Persistent cookies last until you or your browser delete them or until they expire.

Some cookies are associated with your account and Personal Data to remember that you have logged in and what parts of the Service or the Website you have logged into. Other cookies are not tied to your account but are unique and allow us to carry out site analytics and customization, among other similar things. If you access the Service through your browser, you can manage your cookie settings there, but if you disable some or all cookies, you may not be able to use the Service.

Sales Genio sets and accesses its own cookies on domains operated by Sales Genio and its affiliates. Additionally, we use third parties like Google Analytics for website analytics. You may opt out of third-party cookies from Google Analytics on their website.

Currently, we do not recognize or respond to browser-initiated Do Not Track signals, as there is no consistent industry standard for compliance.
